Just a fun little window tennis game I decided to make in the middle of me studying for exams. Procrastination to the MAX.
You're essentially playing tennis against an AI opponent and your goal is to have a high rally time. The theme of it was suppose to be Wimbeldon theme.
Can you beat the AI opponent? No. I wanted the opponent to not be able to lose in order for the player to set a high rally time. aits just a fun little game to play when you dont feel like finishing your actual work.
